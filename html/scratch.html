    Foundations Taught Right

    The Full Time Filmmaker Course developed by professional filmmaker and business leader Parker Walbeck and his team has taught me more about the media world and content creation more than any other resource. A quick tagline of the course is that it provides more value than a semester of film school. As someone who took an intro film class in college I 100% agree with this statement. The course provides wonderful theory, technical, and practical training all online with content continually updating and expanding. It teaches multiple skills sets from editing, to photography, to business management, to social media promotion. Below is a list of just some of the skills I've learned.

    1. Cinematography
      • How to set proper exposure using aperture, shutter speed, and ISO levels
      • Understand the basic elements of shot compsition
      • Different camera techniques such as smooth video transitions and visually popping contrast

    2. Photo & Video editing
      • Moderate working knowlegde of Adobe Premier Pro for video editing
      • Moderate working knowledge of Adobe Lightroom Classic and Affinity Photo for photo editing. Basic experience with Adobe Photoshop
      • Know of how to edit mass photo batches with consistent styles

    3. Content Distribution
      • Know proper video configuration settings to upload to YouTube
      • Know how to stylzie content for social media platforms
      • Experience using social media analytics to enagage with customers
      • Basic web development skills for content distribution
