Quality Education, Practical Experience My Photo Journey

Every since I was you I loved taking pictures and videos of the world around me. I was fascinated by the photography process and wanted to learn all I could about it. At around age 11 I began using the best cmaera technology I had access to: my family's camrecorde and iPod Nano. Over the next decade my technology improved buut more importantly my skills did too.

I took a variety of photography, film, and graphic design classes in middle school and high school to learn the foundations of my knowledge. I was privileged to also gain in-feild practical experience in a variety of settings that took my knowledge and skills to the next level. I bring all of that experience togther to produce products that make people happy!
